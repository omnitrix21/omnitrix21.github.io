Hey, I'm Aarav. Just a regular tech geek from India. Pretty much always had an interest in exploring technology - be it messing around with software, trying out new operating systems, or whatever else to figure out ways to make my devices run smoother. I tend to be buried deep in various technology-related projects, but I still enjoy playing around with gadgets and finding ways to make the digital world better. I'm so much of an open-source software believer that I keep my ears open for any assistance within this community.
